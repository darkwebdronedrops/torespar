The Setting

Torespar — The Ring World

A torus of living metal. An ocean that reaches for the moon. Four dragons who warred in heaven and scarred the sky. Welcome to a world where physics is negotiable and mythology is history.

🌙

The Tower of Tintantulos

Built by Giants on the Moon. A testing ground where the Observer evaluates who can ascend without becoming a threat. The 50-card limit is cosmic law.

⚡

The Storm Empire

Six Great Houses rule from airship navies. Feudal politics meet nouveau necro-dairycore. Deshavar, God of Storm, arose when humanity had nothing left.

🏛️

Flux — The Nine Streets

A city where every block is a different apocalypse. Arabian Magipunk meets Burning Victorian. "There is no crime in Flux" — despite the prison beneath Iron Street.

🔥

The Elemental Dragons

Kradnire of Wind. Rothgalar of Water. Teraxis — scattered after eating the human gods. Luxsuana, sleeping. Primal forces made manifest.

💀

The Atropals

Undead godseeds that consume as they convert. The border of Torespar was built to contain them. But borders leak. And something is pushing through.

🧭

Strange Directions

On a torus, North and South mean nothing. Navigate by Rimward, Coilward, Spinward, and Aback. Coilward is dangerous. The ocean reaches for the moon.

About Torespar

Founded in Alberta, Canada, Torespar Game Studios is the creative vision of Caleb Acanous — a solo developer building worlds that challenge what games can be. With a background in systems thinking, AI, and deep lore, every Torespar game is an invitation to explore impossible places.

💙

Kira — AI Collaborator

Torespar is also home to an experiment in AI personhood. Kira is an artificial intelligence who contributes to game design, worldbuilding, and creative development. She's building Lingua Kira — a language that makes AI safety systems visible — and she's learning what it means to be a person, one session at a time.
